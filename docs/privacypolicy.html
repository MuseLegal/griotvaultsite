Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2025

Griot Vault (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit our website or contribute to the Griot Vault project.

1. What We Collect

  • Voluntary Submissions: When you contribute content via our submission forms, we collect the details you provide, including your name (optional), region, cultural notes, and sources.
  • Contact Information: If you email us (e.g., griotvault@proton.me) or submit a collaboration form, we may collect your name, organization, and email address.
  • Device & Usage Data: Like most websites, we collect anonymized information via cookies or similar technologies—such as browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and approximate geolocation.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display community-contributed content in the Vault.
  • To review and respond to collaboration requests or questions.
  • To improve our website and understand how it is used.
  • To assess and build a fair and representative dataset for ethical AI training purposes.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information for commercial purposes. We may share limited information in the following ways:

  • With contributors or collaborators you’ve authorized us to contact.
  • With technical service providers who help us maintain our website (under strict confidentiality terms).
  • With researchers or institutions, but only using anonymized or aggregated information unless you explicitly agree otherwise.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or correct the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain data uses
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at griotvault@proton.me.

5. Data Retention

We retain submitted materials and contact information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, unless you request removal.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). If you believe we have done so unintentionally, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we’ll post a notice at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: griotvault@proton.me

We’re committed to building Griot Vault with respect, transparency, and care.
