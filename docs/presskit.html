Griot Vault Press Kit

About

Griot Vault is an open-source digital archive preserving the cultural memory and wisdom of the African diaspora in machine-readable formats. Designed for use by researchers, educators, developers, and journalists, it supports both cultural preservation and equitable AI training.

Quick Facts

  • Launched in 2024 as a nonprofit initiative
  • 500+ entries contributed so far — including proverbs, stories, and biographies
  • Free and open to the public
  • Founded in 2024 as a nonprofit initiative
  • Uses Markdown for accessibility and AI-readiness

Press Release

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

New York, NY — Griot Vault has launched a platform for collecting and structuring the cultural legacy of the African diaspora. By organizing oral traditions, proverbs, practices, and more in machine-readable format, the archive helps ensure that Black culture is included in tomorrow’s data-driven tools.

We’re not just preserving the past. We’re structuring it so that AI and other technologies reflect the richness of Black identity and experience.

Key Messages

  • Griot Vault makes African and diasporic knowledge visible, searchable, and shareable.
  • Built by community, for community — and for the future of ethical AI.
  • Stories are formatted to work with both web readers and machine learning tools.
  • Participation is open to individuals, schools, libraries, and institutions.
